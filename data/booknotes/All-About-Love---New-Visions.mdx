### Summary
Our nation is turning away from love and giving messages of its cynical irrelevance. "Love is for the naive, the weak, the hopelessly romantic". Few love writers speak of patriarchy and how domination of woman and children stand in way of love. Seeking a better love requires the difficult task of acknowledging we know and experience little love in our current and past relationships. Love is "the will(an action) to extend one's self for the purpose of nurturing one's own or another's spiritual growth." Abuse and neglect can't coexist with love if its about spiritual growth. Spiritual refers to life force that enhances our ablity to be self actualized and in communion.

Price of presenting false self is loss of their capacity to give and receive love. “widespread cultural acceptance of lying is a primary reason many of us will never know love” “Trust is the foundation of intimacy”. Early feminists expressed desire to know men better but some couldn’t cope and wanted old lies and pretenses. Commitment to truth telling in daily life like after gift seen as freak, but “Lies may make people feel better, but they do not help them to know love”. 

Maxim can’t love others if don’t love self, but you learn to learn self and other in loving environment. “wounded heart learns self love by first overcoming low self esteem” book 6 pillars of self esteem lists living consciously, self acceptance, self responsibility, self assertiveness, living purposefully, and personal integrity. Living consciously is thinking critically about life from actions, purpose, values, and goals. self responsibility is taking responsibility or believing we have the ability to shape our actions, attainment of goals, life, and wellbeing. Living purposely means taking responsibility for consciously aligning with self created goals. “Do not expect to receive the love from someone else you do not give yourself”

Modern New Age dangerously narcissistic focused on self improvement over practice of love within a community. "My belief that God is love - that love is everything, our true destiny - sustains me"
